Step 1:

Playing on iOS or PC? Download an Android emulator.


As you may (or may not) know, the Amazon Appstore only works on Android-enabled devices or Fire tablets. That means you can’t natively sideload the Amazon Appstore onto your iOS enabled device.
Fortunately, all you need is an Android emulator to sidestep this obstacle.
What exactly is an emulator? An emulator is a piece of software that makes it possible to mimic an Android device on a computer running macOS or Windows.

1.    Go to www.bignox.com

2.    Click “Download”

3.    Once downloaded, open the download file to install the Nox App Player

4.    After installation, open the Nox App—note that running the Nox App chews through a lot of memory, so be sure to close all other open apps

https://www.bignox.com/

Step 2:

Download and install Amazon Appstore using your emulator.


The Amazon Appstore is essential if you want to be able to purchase decks and adventures for Hearthstone at a discount.
Once the Nox App is open, paste the URL below into the navigation bar of the browser within the emulator.
http://bit.ly/getcoins102

How to Download and Install the Amazon Appstore


After following the above URL, click “Get Amazon Underground”. This will prompt an .APK file to download onto your emulator.
(This app is built and distributed by Amazon. It is perfectly safe to run on your device.)
When the download is complete, run the .APK file to install the Amazon Appstore.

Step 3:

Open Amazon Appstore on your emulator, sign-in to your Amazon account, and download Hearthstone.


Hearthstone should download automatically, but in case it doesn’t, here are instructions to download Hearthstone within the emulator:

1.    Sign-in to the Amazon Appstore app through the emulator using your Amazon account (if you do not have one, you will need to create one)

2.    Search for “Hearthstone” in the Amazon Appstore app

3.    Find, select, and install Hearthstone

4.    When the installation has finished, open Hearthstone through the Amazon Appstore, or through the Nox emulator

Step 4:

With Hearthstone successfully launched, sign-in to your Blizzard Account.


You now have the “Amazon” version of Hearthstone. That means you can now get exclusive access to Amazon Coins and complete in-app purchases at a discount.

Step 5:

Buy card packs and start saving.


Great! You’re just one step away from using Amazon Coins to start saving on Hearthstone card packs and adventures.

1.    In the Hearthstone app, click “Shop”

2.    Select what you want to purchase and click, “Buy”


Now, when you buy card packs or adventures, you’ll automatically be presented with the option to buy Amazon Coins in bulk and save. The best part? You only need to complete the purchase in the emulator-you don't have to play in the emulator.
Because your purchases will be automatically tied to your Blizzard account, you can log out of Hearthstone on the emulator, log back in using your preferred iOS device, and continue playing as usual.
