Step 3:

Open Amazon Appstore on your emulator, sign-in to your Amazon account, and download Hearthstone.


Hearthstone should download automatically, but in case it doesn’t, here are instructions to download Hearthstone within the emulator:

1.    Sign-in to the Amazon Appstore app through the emulator using your Amazon account (if you do not have one, you will need to create one)

2.    Search for “Hearthstone” in the Amazon Appstore app

3.    Find, select, and install Hearthstone

4.    When the installation has finished, open Hearthstone through the Amazon Appstore, or through the Nox emulator
